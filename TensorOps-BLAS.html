General strategy:

  • We can only outsource to BLAS (using dispatchBLAS) in the case that os and ns have length 0 or 1. Anything else, fall back to the basic reverse-indexing method in Data.Nested.
  • If ms is length 2 or higher, "traverse down" to the length 0 or 1 tail...and then sum them up.
